About NANOFINS

At NANOFINS, our consultancy holds a support mechanism that has engaged research and development among other tools to navigate the trackless terrain of business for innovation, growth, and sustainable success for individuals, firms, and government.

We encourage businesses to discover the foundation of their ideas as an impetus to building a successful and sustainable enterprise. From the ideation stage, our method enables, then empowers entrepreneurs to scale to full bona fide movements (of businesses or institutions).

We form teams with them to comb through their ideas and discover their Value Propositions (VP), which is the foundation. As thought leaders of the subject, you will naturally find us championing this foundation for businesses.

Our consultancy engages various vehicles to achieve success, including online and offline classes using manuals to simulate hands-on experience, short video lessons engaging storytelling to pass on our training sessions, etc.

We want to come through with intentions to influence policy directions to create a favorable environment toward a positive SME Ecosystem.
